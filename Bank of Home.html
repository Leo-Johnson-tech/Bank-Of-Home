Loan Policy

  • Interest Rate: 7%.
  • Loan Disbursement: Half-Yearly.
  • Maximum Loan for Adults: ₹70,000.
  • Maximum Loan for Minors: ₹10,000.
  • Failure to repay on time will result in Raids and Money Recovery.

Rules & Regulations

  1. All borrowers must submit valid Identification.
  2. Loan Repayment must be made within the agreed time frame.
  3. Failure to repay on time will lead to Legal Actions including raids to recover the owed amount.
  4. Minors can only borrow up to ₹10,000 as the principal amount.
  5. Loans are available only for Specific Purposes verified by the bank.
