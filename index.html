Loan Policy

  • Interest Rate: 7%.
  • Loan Disbursement: Half-Yearly.
  • Maximum Loan for Adults: ₹70,000.
  • Maximum Loan for Minors: ₹10,000.
  • Failure to repay on time will result in Raids and Money Recovery.

Rules & Regulations

  1. All borrowers must submit valid Identification.
  2. Loan Repayment must be made within the agreed time frame.
  3. Failure to repay on time will lead to Legal Actions including raids to recover the owed amount.
  4. Minors can only borrow up to ₹10,000 as the principal amount.
  5. Loans are available only for Specific Purposes verified by the bank.

How to Get a Loan?

Getting a loan from Bank of Home is a simple process. Just follow these steps:

  1. Visit Our Website: Go to the Bank of Home website or visit any of our branches.
  2. Fill Out the Application: Complete the loan application form with necessary details.
  3. Submit Documents: Provide valid identification, proof of income, and other required documents.
  4. Loan Approval: Our team will review your application and documents. If approved, you will be notified about the loan amount and repayment schedule.
  5. Receive Funds: Once everything is confirmed, your loan will be disbursed to your bank account or in cash, depending on the loan type.

Our employees will be grateful for your trust in us and are here to assist you at every step of the process!
